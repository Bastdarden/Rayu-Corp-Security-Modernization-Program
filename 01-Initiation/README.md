## Security Modernization Program Charter

This charter formally authorized Rayu Corp's Security Modernization Program and established the strategic foundation for cybersecurity governance, enterprise risk management, vendor risk oversight, security awareness initiatives, executive reporting, and incident response improvements.

### Key Contributions
Defined cybersecurity program vision and objectives
Established governance structure and stakeholder responsibilities
Identified program scope and success criteria
Documented strategic risks and mitigation approaches
Developed implementation milestones and executive oversight framework

### Skills Demonstrated
Cybersecurity Program Management
Governance & Risk Management
Strategic Planning
Executive Reporting
Stakeholder Management
Program Governance

## Executive Business Case & Funding Proposal

Developed a business case supporting investment in the Enterprise Security Modernization Program. The proposal evaluated organizational security challenges, strategic business drivers, program costs, expected benefits, and risks of inaction while providing executive leadership with a structured recommendation for cybersecurity investment.

### Key Contributions
Identified cybersecurity business challenges
Developed executive funding justification
Evaluated program costs and benefits
Assessed organizational risk exposure
Defined measurable success criteria
Supported executive decision-making
