Developed a centralized enterprise risk register to identify, prioritize, and manage cybersecurity risks across the organization. The register established risk ownership, mitigation strategies, scoring methodologies, and governance oversight processes to improve organizational resilience and risk visibility.

Assigned risk ownership and accountability
Defined mitigation and treatment strategies
Established governance reporting metrics
Improved executive visibility into risk posture

## Vendor Risk Assessment

Conducted a third-party vendor security assessment to evaluate cybersecurity controls, operational resilience, and risk exposure associated with a critical cloud services provider. Assessed security practices, documented findings, assigned risk ratings, and developed remediation recommendations to improve vendor oversight and reduce organizational risk.

### Key Contributions
Evaluated third-party security controls
Assessed vendor operational risks
Identified control gaps and remediation actions
Assigned risk ratings
Supported vendor governance decisions
Established ongoing monitoring requirements
